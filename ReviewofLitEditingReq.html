Review of the Literature Editing Requirements:

  1. Complete your first draft

  2. Print out a copy of the Adult Review sheet (link on calendar)

  3. Find an adult (not a parent or sibling) and ask them kindly if they will read your first draft and complete the survey.

  4. Modify your 1st draft incorporating the suggestions into your 2nd draft (do not discard the first draft or survey).

  5. Print out an new copy of your 2nd draft and an adult survey, find a 2nd different adult (again not a parent or sibling) willing to read and comment on your 2nd draft.

  6. Modify your 2nd draft.... print the 3rd version and turn everything in clipped together on the due date 2nd semester.

Helpful Hints:

  1. Don't wait until the last minute.

  2. Set a reasonable date of completion with the adult (generally 2-3 days).

  3. Be polite and courteous and say thank you.
