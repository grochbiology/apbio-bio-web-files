Organism Inquiry Lab

Procedure:

Day 1  (1) Observe the organism.  (2) Write observations in journal.  (3) Write questions you’d like to ask in your journal.  (4) Share questions symposium.  (5) Form groups of 3-4 students based on question categories.   (6) Decide on one question you’d like to get answered.  ( 7) Develop an experiment that could answer the question. (8) Write a prediction for the outcome of your experiment  (9) Decide who will bring the various materials for experiment to the next class

Day 2:  (1)  Gather group members and materials.  (2) Set up experiment   (3) Label experiment with Class period #, Table #  (4) Store experiment in the assigned location in the lab room. 

Day 3:  (1) Gather group members   (2) Observe your experiment and write observations in journal.*create a data table.    (3)  Discuss your results  (4)  Individually write formal lab report.
