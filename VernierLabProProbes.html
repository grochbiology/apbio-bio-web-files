Carbon Dioxide Gas (8); Cellular Respiration, Cell Respiration & Photosynthesis, Cell Respiration & Photosynthesis with both probes

Temperature (12): Dissolved Oxygen in Water; Aerobic Respiration

pH (12): New Behavior Lab

Colorimeters (4) with 4 fixed wavelengths: Photosynthesis, Population Dynamics, Effect of Alcohol on Biological Membranes, Biological Membranes

violet (430 nm), blue (470 nm), green (565 nm), and red (635 nm).
