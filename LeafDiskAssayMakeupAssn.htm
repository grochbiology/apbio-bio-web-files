5. Compare the rates of the trendlines. For example the reaction rate is equal to the slope which is "m" this value is 1.67 at 100% (13 cm) of light,  the rate at 50% (18 cm) is 1.43, the rate at 25% (26 cm) is 1.02, and the rate at 12.5% (35 cm) is 0.92. Here is some example calculations and interpretations.

    1.67/0.92= 1.82; the photosynthetic rate is nearly 2x greater than at 12.5% of light intensity

    1.67/1.43= 1.17; the rate at 13 cm and 18 cm (100% to 50%) light intensity is nearly the same...
