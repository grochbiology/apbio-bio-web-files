Phylum Presentation Instructions:  

1. Your group is responsible for presenting  information for your phylum. You may present in a PowerPoint, Prezi or other electronic form. Be creative and do something different if it works for you.
Key to an A grade:
2. NO MORE THAN 5 BULLET POINTS WITH NO MORE THAN 5 WORDS PER BULLET PER SLIDE, please!!!  Just the bare facts please. You can put extra information in the fact sheet you emailed out
3.  Make sure the text is clearly visable and large enough to be seen at the back of the classroom.
4. If you are using a MAC, do NOT paste pictures directly into your presentation, MACs save those pictures as TIFF files which the school PC does not recognize. Please save your images to your MAC and then insert picture.
5. Label your images with titles.
6. Cite your images in one of 2 ways; a) the URL below the image OR b) a number and create a pictures cited slide at the end of your presentation.
7. Cite all your references in APA style (use :www.bibme.org) in a slide at the end of your presentation.
8. If you are doing a PREZI presentation, make sure your transitions are NOT too fast.
9. This information will/may be included on upcoming unit tests. You will be graded on the quality of your presentation, preparedness, depth and accuracy. Teachers discretion :-).  

Reminder: Email your presentation and information sheet to the class email address so everyone can have access.  (The class email address and password will be given out in class).
