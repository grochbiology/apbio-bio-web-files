Final Presentation Rubric:

This assignment is worth 100 points.

Point Breakdown:

25 points for group summary sheet with references ( teacher)

25 points for individual/group presentations (teacher)

25 points for individual participation in discussions (teacher)

25 points for Group Participation Sheet (group) Make sure you fill this out and turn in by due date, see calendar.

Additional Information

1. Group presentations are expected to look as professional as possible. Student presenters are expected to act as professional as possible (avoid giggling, laughing, talking to the board) and role playing is expected (students should present in "character".) If you plan on using the LCD, I need to know a day in advance.

2. Audience members are expected to be in "character" and act accordingly. Any disturbances that are "out of character" will result in a reduction of points for the assignment. Inappropriate disruptions include; talking, off task, working on presentations, etc.

3. Each stakeholder group must submit a summary of the planned presentation on the day that the group goes in front of the council (this includes the "city council" and "mayor".)

4. The groups will have a large portion of the period  to present their reports and need to leave at least 10 minutes to answer questions. Write down any questions to be asked in your journal so you don't interrupt the presenters. Students are expected to ask at least 2 in-depth well thought out questions during the 4 days of presentations to earn participation credit.
