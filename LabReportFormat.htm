Sample Lab Report (Title should =lab # and short description)

 

 

Purpose: What is the point of the lab, what are you trying to learn, why it is interesting, etc. Approx. 1 paragraph

 

Hypothesis: The lab may say in it what it is trying to prove, if so, summarize in your own words. If not, what do you think the results of the lab will be? 1-2 sentences to 1 paragraph.

 

Procedure: Summarize all the steps of the lab. Be detailed, but don’t exactly copy the lab manual. I should be able to repeat the lab based on you notes in this section.

              

               Also include any pictures, graphs, measurements, tables, etc. made during the lab in this section

              

               This section is to be as long as is necessary to include all information.

 

Observations: Use your senses! What did you see, hear, smell (of appropriate), taste (if appropriate), and touch (if appropriate) during this lab. Include any observations you have, you never know what will be important when you start to draw conclusions! This section should also be as long as is necessary to include all information. I t should be at least 1-2 paragraphs.

 

Conclusion: What did your observations lead you to believe about your hypothesis? Did it prove it, disprove, not affect it? What experimental error factors may have played a part (contamination, measurements, human error, methods, materials, etc.)? What could be done in a future lab to better prove or disprove the hypothesis and limit experimental error? What other questions did this lab raise? This section should be at least 1-2 paragraphs as well.
