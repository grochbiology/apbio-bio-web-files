Four Trait Cross (Tetrahybrid)

Directions:  To do your cross:

a.  Choose anything to cross (shoes, super heroes, animals etc...)

b.  Each crossed "thing" must be heterozygous for a minimum of two of its traits

c.  Determine the 4 traits you will cross and their alleles. (use letters that can easily be distinguished apart E/e, H/h, T/t, etc...)

d.  Draw and label the parts ( see the mouse example below) of the things you will cross.  (drawings receive more pts and are better than photocopies)

e.  Use product rule for your cross and neatly organize and show all of your work.  No punnet squares.

f.  Box your genotypes and phenotypes in your final answer

g.  Draw the F1 generation

h.  Final product must be neat, on plain white paper, organized and in color.

i.  Incorrect, poorly organized, sloppy, rushed papers will not be considered for grading.
