Unit Exam: Nervous System and Behavior ( Ch 28 and 37.11-37.22) Study Guide

This will be a short answer test (no multiple choice)

  1. Describe the role of sensory neurons, interneurons, and motor neurons in a reflex arc.
  2. What are roles of Na+, Ca+, and K+ in nerve impulse transmission?
  3. Describe how a neurotransmitter initiates an action potential. What can interfere or prevent action potentials?
  4. Explain why the parasympathetic and sympathetic divisions of the autonomic nervous system are antagonists of each other. Give an example of one set of antagonists.
  5. Describe the main functions of the cerebrum, pons, hippocampus, medulla oblongata and the thalamus.
  6. What is sociobiology?
  7. Explain how altruistic acts can be explained by evolution.
