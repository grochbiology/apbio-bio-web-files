AP Biology Body Systems Structure & Function Presentation Assignment

from http://ww2.tyrrell.k12.nc.us/webumake/staff/clevis/APBioBodySystems.html

 

The following assignment will count as a test grade:

 

1.)   Research information on your assigned body system.  Use your textbook AND at least one other source.  Cite any additional sources that you have used on a works cited page.

 

2.)   Prepare a detailed outline of your body system.  This should include the major tissues/organs and their structure and function, as well as how all of the parts contribute to the holistic functioning of your particular system.  Your outline should NOT exceed the front and back of a page, but it must be at least one page- 12 pt. font maximum.  You will need to make copies of your outline to distribute to the class via email to save paper (make sure you cite any images that you "borrow"; Google images, etc.).  This outline will serve are their basis of system information.  Make sure that you provide enough detail so that your classmates will have a study aid for the final exam.

 

3.)   Prepare a power-point presentation that will provide a through explanation of your assigned system.  You will need to provide visuals of particular parts of your system in your presentation.  This presentation needs to be at least five minutes in length.  Please test your presentation on the classroom equipment prior to your date of presentation.  You may do this after school.  No consideration will be given for ‘technical difficulties’ that result from lack of preparation.

 

4) Chapters will be split up into 2 to 3 individual sections. Each student is responsible for their own presentation. There will be no group presentations for this project.
