Making a Bar Graph using Excel 2007

1.       Organize your data in Excel.

 

2.       Highlight data, go to Insert, select Column data

3.       Select the Layout Options that gives you a title and axes (Layout 6 or 9)

4.        

 

5.       Should look like this now:

6.       Right click on the body of the graph and select “select data” from the pop up window

 

7.        Click on the x axis information (salt drops in this example), then click remove.  Then click on Edit of the Horizontal (Category) Axis Labels….

8.       The Axis labels box will pop up, highlight the x axis labels and then click okay twice to close both pop up windows.

 

9.       Label the title and axes by clicking on the titles and editing.

 

 

10.   Copy and paste into your lab write up.
