SELL THAT ORGANELLE !!! (Extra Credit 5 points)

Objective:  To create a 1 minute commercial for your assigned organelle.

During the course of your commercial, you should provide:

A. the organelle's shape and function (be sure to consider their relationship to each other) 

B. a diagram of the organelle

C. a catchy slogan

D. the type of cell(s) associated with your organelle

E. any other distinguishing characteristics (at least 2) *possibly which other organelles it works closely with in terms of function *possibly whether it is part of the endomembrane system    * possibly if it IS or IS NOT located in all cells *etc....

THIS information should be presented as part of the commercial.  You will need to turn in a typed script for the commercial
