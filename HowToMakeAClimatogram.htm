How to Make a Climatogram using Excel(to be included in your Power Point presentation)

 

  1. Locate the correct data for your biome.  Consult a map or atlas as needed.
  2. Open Microsoft Excel
  3. Enter your data in columns.

 

Like this:

Month

Precipitation (cm)

Temperature (oC)

J

12.8

4.6

F

11.9

3.8

 

  1. Once your data is inputted, you are ready to graph the data. 

·              Select all cells with data, including headings.

·              Go to the chart wizard (it looks like a small bar graph with red, blue and yellow bars).

·              Choose custom types

·              Choose line-column on 2 axes

·              Choose next

·              Choose next

·              Give the chart a title, and label the x-axis, y-axis (prec), and secondary y-axis (temp).  Note:  the bars should be precipitation, and the line should be temperature.  If not, go back and check to make sure that your table looks exactly like the one above. Choose next.

·              Choose finish.

·              Once the chart has been formed in your table, double click on the left Y-axis numbers.

·              Click on scale at the top.  Change the scale to MIN 0, MAX 25, MAJOR UNIT 1.  Click ok.

·              Double click on the right Y-axis numbers.  Click on scale at the top, and change the values to MIN 20, MAX 30, MAJOR UNIT 1.  Click ok.

·              Now you need to size your chart so that it looks correct, and will be the same for all presentations.  Click once on the outer edge of the graph.  This should cause eight black boxes to appear around the outside of the graph.  Go to one at the corner, click and hold on that box.  Drag the graph out until it is approximately 7 columns wide by 30 rows long.

·              Save the file.

 

  1. To insert the graph into your power point presentation:

·              Select the entire graph by clicking on the outside rim.  Choose copy from the Edit menu.

·              Open the power point presentation, and go the slide where you want the climatogram.  Under the Edit menu choose Paste.

·              At this point, you can move or adjust the climatogram as you can with any image.

·              Make sure to include this in your presentation.
