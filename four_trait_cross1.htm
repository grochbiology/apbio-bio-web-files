Four Trait Cross (Tetrahybrid)

Directions:  To do your cross:

a.  Choose anything to cross (shoes, super heroes, animals etc...)

b.  Each crossed "thing" must be heterozygous for a minimum of two of its traits and have one X linked trait.  (an example genotype would look like... AaBbccXWY or aaBBCcXWXw)***

c.  Determine the 4 traits you will cross and their alleles. (use letters that can easily be distinguished apart E/e, H/h, T/t,  and use proper X linked trait notation  etc...)

d.  Draw, color and label the parts ( see the mouse example below) of the things you will cross.  (drawings receive more pts and are better than photocopies)

e.  Use product rule for your cross and neatly organize and show all of your work.  No punnet squares.

f.  Box your genotypes and phenotypes in your final answer

g.  Draw the F1 generation phenotype for each offspring (colored, etc). Make sure you state whether they are male or female.

h.  Final product must be neat, on plain white paper, organized and in color.

i.  Incorrect, poorly organized, sloppy, rushed papers will not be handed back for redo without grading).

****(Please note that the example is missing the XX or XY traits from the picture this student did only autosomal traits and neglected to list which of the probable offspring are male or female... example only useful  for a general idea)
