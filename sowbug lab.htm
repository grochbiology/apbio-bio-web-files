Sowbug (Pillbug, rollie-pollies)  Behavior Lab

 

Purpose: Determining sowbug  preferences

 

Materials provided:

 

Two to four part choice chamber (opaque plastic box)

10 sowbugs

 

Materials available:

Filter paper

Foil

Sand

Acid (0.1 M HCl)

Base (0.1 NaOH)

Pipets

Any other materials must be supplied by student lab group

 

Directions: Each lab group will develop a testable question in the form of a hypothesis (If… then… because… format) regarding sowbug preferences. Design an experiment to test the sowbugs’ preferences; one to four sides of choice chamber containing the experimental variable, the other side of choice chamber containing the control set up. Each group will place 10 sowbugs in the control side and then note the number of sowbugs on each side every minute for 10 minutes.
