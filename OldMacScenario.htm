Old MacDonald's Pigs Scenario PBL

 

Sam MacDonald has a small shareholding that has been in the MacDonald family for 300 years. The farm doesn't pay for itself, so Sean had to take a job at the factory in town. Still, he does plant and he built a piggery from one of the ancient outbuildings on the property. He hired a person to take care of things while he was at work.

 

One day, after Sean went to work, the pump that supplied water to the piggery broke down.  The hired man spent the entire day finding the parts and repairing the pump.  When Sean came home that evening, he checked in on the pigs. They were all showing signs of illness. Many of them were on the ground, in convulsions. Sean quickly ran into this house and called you, his veterinarian.

 

Your job is to determine what is happening to the pigs, why is it happening, and what treatment is most appropriate to use to save Farmer MacDonald's pigs.
