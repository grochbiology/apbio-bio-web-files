Classroom Etiquette: Respect on Responsibility

 

 

1)      Respect your fellow classmates and your classroom

a)     Keep your hands, feet and other objects to yourself.

b)    Use appropriate language and behavior in the classroom.

c)     Do not interrupt a classmate or the teacher when they are speaking.

2)    Be Responsible

a) Enter the classroom quietly and on time.

b)    Sit in your assigned seat.

c)     Bring all required materials with you to class everyday.

d)     Have all assignments completed on time.

e)     Be prepared for all evaluations and lab procedures.
