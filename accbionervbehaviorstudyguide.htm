Nervous system and Behavior Test Study Guide  (This is a short answer test, there are 7 questions that look just like these on the test)

  1. Describe the role of sensory neurons, interneurons, and motor neurons in a reflex arc.

  2. What are roles of Na+, Ca+, and K+ in nerve impulse transmission? Diagram and explain the path of nerve impulse transmission through a neuron.

  3. Describe how a neurotransmitter initiates an action potential. What can interfere or prevent action potentials?

  4. Explain why the parasympathetic and sympathetic divisions of the autonomic nervous system are antagonists of each other. Give an example of one set of antagonists.

  5. Describe the main functions of the cerebrum, pons, hippocampus, medulla oblongata and the thalamus. Explain the possible role of long term potentiation in memory and learning. (you will have to look this up, not in 6th Edition of your textbook).

  6. What is sociobiology? How does sociobiology affect evolutionary fitness? Give one example.

  7. Explain how altruistic acts can be explained by evolution.
