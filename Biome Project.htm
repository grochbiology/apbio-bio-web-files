Group Assignment:  PBL:  Design a travel brochure for an assigned biome. Below you will find the directions, due date, and online help sites for this project.  Good luck and happy traveling!

 You are the travel agents.  Your company is running a contest to determine the group that can sell the most trips to one of the earth's biomes. Your reward is a huge bonus and a vacation to anywhere in the world.  Your brochure must:

  •  Be colorful, eye catching, accurate.
  •  It must tell a person what they will see in terms of the biotic factors:  dominant species of plant, animal,
  • Include a typical food chain (they might even have to become part of the food chain if food is scarce),
  • Include what to wear during the trip so be sure to include the abiotic factors
  • Include a typical climatogram for the region. 
  • Determine the primary limiting factors of your biome
  • Determine the effect of human impact
  • Include a 5 question quiz on the major features of your biome
  • XC credit if you and members of your group come dressed to visit the biome on the due date:  May 31st.  *If I can guess what your biome is by the way you are dressed....you earn the extra credit*
