Fair Day  San Ramon Valley High School (Commons/Cafeteria)

*Parents: Feed your child a good breakfast.  Provide your child with a water bottle to keep at their project area during judging. Do not provide snacks please.

SA will provide fruit and healthy snacks to keep the energy level high.

*9:30-10:30 am Buddies will check in and locate their project table #

Tables will be set up by Project Category. 

Project numbers will be on the tables at the section set aside for the buddy project.

Buddies and Parents will set up their projects at their assigned section.

*Parents are asked to leave the gym promptly at 10:30 before judging begins.

Parents may return at 1:15 PM to look at projects

*Buddies should remain with their projects at all times once the project is set up until judging is over at around 12:00

*There will be 3 judges for each project in each category.

Buddies should not leave their project until they have been judged by the final judge. 

Each judge will mark the JUDGED slip attached to the project board.

  If a buddy has not been judged 3 times the buddy should inform the SA officer in charge of their section.

If a buddy must leave their project for a rest room break, they will inform the SA officer in charge of their section.  Buddies should return promptly.  

*12:15-1:00 Lunch for Buddies (catered lunch provided)

SA officers  will tally points while eating lunch.

*1:00-2:15  Activities... Mentors/Buddies enjoy FUN activities in the theatre patio outside the commons

SA officers  will continue to tally points and make award selections

 *2:30- 3:15 PM Awards Ceremony (either big gym (if raining) or patio between commons and PAC)

Every project will receive an award. 

Awards are 1st through 3rd place.
