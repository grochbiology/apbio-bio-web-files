Characteristics of the Animal Kingdom

(Adapted from BSCS Blue 2001/ Lab Investigation 18)

Objective: to observe the external characteristics of various phyla of the animal kingdom.

Directions: Carefully examine the Animal phyla survey bottles. The specimens are very fragile. Please handle very carefully; do not shake. Observe the animals, record the common names in the correct column, and record your observations in the tables below.
