Extra Credit Article Reviews:

Requirements: Pick one of the articles listed below, read the article and type a one page summary of the findings, information and the importance to humans in the article. Please* include the author, title of article, journal, date published and pages of the article at the bottom of your review. Each article review is worth 5 points of extra credit. You can earn up to 20 points per quarter (total 40 points per semester).

 

(* Reference Format: Last Name of Author, First Name, Initial (include all authors). (Year). "Title of Article." Journal Name. Volume; pages.)
