Mini-Posters:

Here are links to instructions about making mini-posters instead of full lab reports. You are using 2 file folders (overlapping) to create a mini-poster (science fair board). You must use the “headings with prompts” on your group board. Half the class will present while the other half will act as “judges” using the rubric and then you will switch.
