National Honor Society

Based upon guidelines set down in

The National Honor Society National Constitution

MEMBERSHIP AGREEMENT

 

ARTICLE I. – Pledge of Membership

I pledge to maintain high scholastic standing by having a grade point average of 3.5 or above, to give myself freely in service of others, to endeavor intelligently and courageously to be a leader, and to hold as fundamental and worthy an untarnished character. In so doing, I shall prove myself worthy of a place in the National Honor Society.

 

ARTICLE II. – Guidelines for Maintaining Membership

I agree to attend meetings and participate in community service activities. In order to remain a member in good standing and earn a sash for graduation I need to earn a minimum of 10 points per year. These points are earned by attending meeting (8 meetings = 1 point) and by participating in active and passive service/community projects (1 point per activity/day). Members must have completed all 10 points by May  (Seniors must have completed 8 of 10 points by April 30) to remain members in good standing. I will also have completed an individual service project (ISP) that benefits both the school and the community. I also agree to pay the $10 in club dues by the 2nd meeting of the year ( a portion of the dues will go towards a SRVHS NHS scholarship and offset the cost of the NHS stole for graduating seniors).

 

ARTICLE III. –Specifications for the Individual Service Project (ISP)

The individual service project undertaken must consume at least thirty classroom/working hours. I must not receive any form of compensation for rendered services as per national guidelines. I will submit a one page proof-of-completion form (available online) which includes a brief summary of the service project, what I learned from the project, and a candid evaluation by the coordinator of the service project ISP by Thursday, May 24 (Senior ISP due April 30, 2007).

 

ARTICLE IV. – School Policy

I also pledge not to engage in any activity that is in violation of school policy (i.e., cheating,  plagiarism, drinking, etc…). Violation of school policy may result in my dismissal from SRVHS NHS.

 

ARTICLE V. –Statement of Understanding

By choosing to hold myself to the standards that have been set forth, I pledge myself to be an active member of the San Ramon Valley High School National Honor Society.  I understand that failing to meet the obligations above I will be ineligible to wear the NHS sash during graduation senior year. I understand it is my responsibility to keep track of my standing and inform my parents or guardians of my ineligibility should I fail to complete my obligations. I understand that I may withdraw my membership at any time knowing that I cannot ever be re-admitted. I also understand that failure to adhere to these guidelines will result in my removal from the organization by the faculty council.  (Please keep a copy for your records).
